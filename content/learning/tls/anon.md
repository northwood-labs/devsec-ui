## Summary

Anonymous key exchanges are generally vulnerable to [Man-in-the-Middle attacks][MITM].

Any cipher suite with `anon` in the name should be avoided.

[MITM]: https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Man-in-the-middle_attack
